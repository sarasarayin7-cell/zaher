How We Work

A professional copy trading flow built around daily signal access, controlled execution windows, market-session timing, and simple performance tracking from one clean dashboard.

1. Activate the account

Users activate their account by funding it with USDT BEP20. After activation, the dashboard unlocks the daily copy trading area, balance tracking, profit overview, and withdrawal tools.

2. Receive a private daily copy code

Each trading day, the system prepares a unique copy code for the user. The code is designed for one-time use only and closes after the daily trading cycle, keeping every session controlled and organized.

3. Copy, paste, and follow the signal

The user copies the generated trade code, pastes it into the copy trading panel, and follows the signal timing. This creates an experience similar to larger copy trading companies where users follow structured market opportunities instead of manually searching for every entry.

4. One trade per day with flexible timing

Each user is allowed to trade once per day. A trade can last between 1 minute and 30 minutes, sometimes slightly more or less depending on when the user pastes the copy code, the selected opportunity, liquidity, and live market movement.

5. Timing follows global sessions

Signal behavior can change according to open global markets, including the European session, the American session, and other active exchange periods. Stronger liquidity, volatility, and market participation can influence how fast a copied trade reaches its target window.

6. Track profit and trading progress

After the copy trade cycle, the dashboard helps users follow total balance, daily profit, growth progress, and trading volume. The system keeps the workflow simple: activate, copy the daily opportunity, track results, and unlock smoother withdrawals.
